Dill Gazpacho

Dish Type: Cold Vegetarian Soup / Appetizer

Info

  • Prep Time:: 25 mins
  • Additional Time:: 1 hr
  • Total Time:: 1 hr 25 mins
  • Servings:: 6
  • Yield:: 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 6 medium ripe tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 cucumbers, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1 large lemon, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh dill

Directions

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients
  2. Step 2: In a large bowl, stir together tomatoes, cucumber, onion, bell pepper, and jalapeno pepper. Season with lemon juice, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt and pepper
  3. Step 3: In a blender or food processor, puree half of the mixture until smooth
  4. Step 4: Return to bowl, stir in dill and mix well. Cover and chill for at least one hour before serving
